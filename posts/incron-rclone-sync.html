incron and rclone for syncing screenshots to google drive on linux

I frequently take screenshots in my laptop & use them to recall what I had read and worked on (kind of like Anki notes). I wanted to automatically sync my screenshots to google-drive to look at my laptop screenshots on my phone when I am on the go.

Since ubuntu automatically stores screenshots in Pictures folder, To automatically watch for new file creation in Pictures folder I used incron(inode notify cron) and to sync them to google-drive I used rclone.

  • To set-up rclone for google-drive, refer to instructions at rclone.org/drive/.
  • To setup incrontab, refer to instructions here
    In short, 
    $ incrontab -e
    Add the following line 
    /home/jkl/Pictures IN_CREATE /usr/bin/rclone sync /home/jkl/Pictures google:photos
